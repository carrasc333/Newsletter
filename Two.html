A moral Story: A turkey was chatting with a bull. "I would love to be able to get to the top of that tree," sighed the turkey, "but I haven't got the energy." The bull replies, "why don't you nibble on some of my droppings because they are packed with nutrients". The turkey pecked at a lump of dung, and found it actually gave him enough strength to reach the lowest branch of the tree. The next day, after eating some more dung, he reached the second branch. Finally after a fourth night, the turkey was proudly perched at the top of the tree. He was promptly spotted by a farmer, who shot him out of the tree.Moral of the story: Bullshit might get you to the top, but it won't keep you there.

Liquor, Wine & Beer

American Harvest Vodka

American Harvest is organic vodka to which a proprietary blend of organic ingredients has been added, creating a truly unique vodka specialty. It is a revolutionary way to look at the vodka category. The addition of these ingredients results in a distinctive, smooth, clean and crisp character.

Kru 82 Vodka

Kru 82 Vodka is made from select French winter wheat and extra pure spring water to create a remarkable tasting vodka. At five times distilled, Kru 82 received a 92 rating from tasting panel for its smooth taste and polished finish.Packaged in a recyclable stainless steel container and glass bottle.

Butterfly Kiss

A light and refreshing tasting rose-style pinot grigio. A less guilty take on strawberry shortcake. Also silver medal winner at the Sunset Magazine Wine competition.

Augey Red Bordeaux

This Cabernet Sauvignon has aromas of ripe black fruits and flavors of blackberry and luscious fruit notes developing into spicy black cherry and hints of cured meats and black tea.

Angry Orchard Cider

This crisp and refreshing cider offers sweet apple notes up front with a subtle dryness at the finish for a balanced cider taste. A hint of Fuji apples adds a layer of complexity and brings out a fresh apple aroma and slightly sweet, ripe apple flavor.

2XIPA

An India Pale Ale kicked up a notch to form a true Double IPA: feverishly hoppy with a malty backbone and higher-than-standard alcohol content. Citrusy hops tease the senses with aromatics and lingering bitterness ,while just the right balance of malts disguises 2XIPAs extra gravity. Double your expectations, this is an ale that demands reverence.
